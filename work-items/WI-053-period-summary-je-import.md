# WI-053: Period-summary Journal Entry import for trend reporting (OPTIONAL — recommended)
**Phase:** 2   **Type:** DATA   **Size:** M
**Blocked by:** WI-003, WI-004, WI-035   **Blocks:** final disabling of FYs 2025/2026 (WI-030 holds them open pending this item)

## Why
With full historical migration out of scope (rule 8), ERPNext's P&L/Sales Analytics would show a cliff before 2027. **Recommendation: YES, do it, scoped to 24 monthly JEs (2025-01 through 2026-12)** — two years is enough for month-over-month and year-over-year comparisons at cutover+1; a third year adds 12 JEs of keying for fast-diminishing value. Sized M.

## Native-first check
Native Journal Entry + native Profit and Loss Statement (Monthly periodicity) / Sales Analytics render the trend once data exists — no custom report. Verdict: native.

## Preconditions
- QBO monthly P&L exports for 2025/2026 (by month, by account) from the WI-003 close packages.
- The 'Historical P&L Offset' equity account exists (WI-004 chart).
- Poster holds the `role_allowed_for_frozen_entries` role ('Accounts Manager') because posting dates fall on/before the frozen date set in WI-035 — verified mechanism in month_end_close.py/Company fields.
- FYs 2025/2026 still enabled (WI-030 holds them open pending this item).

## Scope
- 24 Journal Entries, one per month, posting_date = month-end: one line per new-chart income/expense account (mapped from QBO P&L via coa_mapping.csv), balanced by a single line to 'Historical P&L Offset'. `is_opening='No'`; user_remark = 'QBO monthly P&L summary <YYYY-MM>'. Load via Data Import.
- Equity-neutrality argument (why this cannot corrupt the opening position): each JE nets P&L accounts against the offset equity account; at any balance-sheet date the offset balance and the retained earnings generated by those same P&L lines cancel within Equity, so the Balance Sheet equals the WI-032/WI-033 opening position exactly while P&L reports gain monthly history.
- No party, project, or tax lines (trend only, deliberately).

## Acceptance criteria
- `SELECT COUNT(*) FROM \`tabJournal Entry\` WHERE docstatus=1 AND user_remark LIKE 'QBO monthly P&L summary%'` = 24.
- Balance Sheet as of 2027-01-01 byte-identical before vs after the load (equity total unchanged; assets/liabilities untouched).
- Native P&L (Monthly, FY2026) matches the QBO 2026 P&L within $1 per month.

## Rollback
Cancel the 24 JEs (filter on the user_remark tag).

## Explicitly NOT in this work item
Transaction-level history, AR/AP history, project-level historical profitability (accepting this limitation is the point of a summary import); a third year (raise separately if leadership insists).
